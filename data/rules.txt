Server Rules!
Please read these before posting in any channel.
Most rule breaches will result in a warning, but repeated offense may result in a temporary mute, or even a ban.

-No Harassment, abuse, hate speech or any kind of discriminatory speech
-No excessive cursing
-No explicit (18+) content
-No chat spamming
-No using the @everyone/ @here pings excessively/without permission
-Keep content to its appropriate channel
-No advertising non-TTRPG related products/services
-Keep adventure spoilers to a minimum
-If you have a problem with another person on the subreddit,  speak to the mods, but don't call the person out.

These are preliminary rules, more may or may not be added in the future.
